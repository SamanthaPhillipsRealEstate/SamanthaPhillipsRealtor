Beautifully updated end-unit townhouse in the heart of Royal Oak! This rare 3-level condo offers 3 spacious bedrooms, 2.5 bathrooms, and a 2-car attached garage, all with the added privacy of a private entry.

The main level features convenient first-floor laundry, while the second level showcases an open-concept layout with hardwood floors throughout the kitchen, living, and dining areas—perfect for both everyday living and entertaining. The kitchen is equipped with stainless steel appliances, and a washer and dryer are included for added convenience.

Enjoy serene park views right from the primary bedroom, creating a peaceful retreat. The third level offers three generously sized bedrooms, two full bathrooms, and walk-in closets for ample storage.

Located in a private community with its own parking lot and a quiet sidewalk leading directly to 11 Mile, you're just a short walk from Downtown Royal Oak's vibrant scene—featuring theaters, restaurants, bars, and shopping.

A perfect blend of comfort, style, and location—this is Royal Oak living at its best!
