Lakefront & Canal Frontage – Pointe West, Sand Point

An extraordinary opportunity to build your dream home on 100 feet of premium lakefront along the pristine sandy shores of Saginaw Bay. Situated in the highly sought-after Pointe West Community on Michigan's Gold Coast in Sand Point, Huron County, this property offers exceptional value and an unmatched lifestyle.

With 100.94 feet of direct lake frontage plus canal frontage directly across the street, you'll enjoy the rare combination of breathtaking open-water views and safe, protected boat docking.

This site condominium property provides the flexibility and privacy of a custom home while offering the benefits of a well-maintained community. Perfectly located just ~2 hours from Detroit, East Lansing, or Ann Arbor, it's the ultimate setting for a year-round residence or summer retreat.

Experience the best of both worlds—direct sandy beach access and protected canal docking—making this a true boater's paradise.

Note: Buyer to pay tap fee to Caseville Township at the start of construction.
