Experience luxury pond-front living in this beautifully remodeled colonial in Shelby Township's sought-after Diegel Farms community. Built in 2012 and extensively updated in 2024, this exceptional 4-bedroom, 3.5-bath home offers over 5,000 square feet of finished living space, including a walkout lower level designed for entertaining. Enjoy breathtaking water views from nearly every window and a lifestyle that perfectly blends elegance, comfort, and recreation.

The heart of the home is the newly remodeled gourmet kitchen featuring porcelain countertops, an oversized island, spacious pantry, and a stylish wraparound butler's bar that flows seamlessly into the formal dining room and breakfast nook. High-end finishes and timeless design elements are showcased throughout the home, while the dramatic double staircase leads to four spacious bedrooms, each with walk-in closets.

The luxurious primary suite offers a private spa-like bath and an expansive walk-in closet. Additional highlights include upper-level laundry, a custom mudroom with direct garage access, and a 3-car attached garage. The finished walkout lower level is ideal for entertaining with a full kitchen, and separate bar with sitting area, and flexible living space perfect for gatherings or relaxing nights at home.

Step outside to enjoy the beautifully maintained swimmable pond, ideal for fishing, paddle boating, and canoeing. Located within the award-winning Utica Community Schools and close to shopping, dining, and everyday conveniences, this home offers the perfect combination of upscale living and serene waterfront surroundings.
