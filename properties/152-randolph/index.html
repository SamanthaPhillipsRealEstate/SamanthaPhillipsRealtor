Step into timeless elegance with this stunning 2,700 sq ft Colonial that perfectly blends classic charm with modern luxury. Featuring 4 spacious bedrooms and 2.1 beautifully updated baths, this home offers exceptional comfort and thoughtful upgrades throughout.

The heart of the home is the updated kitchen, showcasing granite countertops, premium JennAir appliances, an Italian-made Smeg dishwasher, warming drawer, and a striking stainless farmhouse sink—all complemented with quality finishes. Plantation-style shutters and solid core doors add sophistication across every room.

A standout feature is the expansive primary suite addition, designed as a private retreat with a spa-like Euro shower, jetted tub, walk-in closet, and its own dedicated heating and cooling system for ultimate comfort.

Upstairs, enjoy the convenience of a second-floor laundry, while all bathrooms have been tastefully updated to reflect modern style and function. Major mechanical updates include a new furnace (2018), A/C (2017), and a brand-new roof (2024), along with upgraded 200-amp electrical service.

Step outside to your own resort-style backyard oasis—perfect for entertaining or relaxing. The fiberglass saltwater pool with slide is the centerpiece, surrounded by a firepit and a beautifully updated pool house (2020) complete with a kitchenette. A stunning three-season room offers additional space to unwind and enjoy the view year-round.

This home truly has it all—luxury, functionality, and unforgettable outdoor living. Hot tub excluded from sale.
