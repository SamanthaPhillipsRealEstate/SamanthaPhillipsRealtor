Welcome to this exceptional pond-front colonial in the highly sought-after Shelby Township community of Diegel Farms, offering breathtaking water views from nearly every window. Located within the award-winning Utica Community Schools, this 5-bedroom, 4.5-bath residence delivers over 3,600 square feet of refined living space plus an additional 1,500+ square feet of beautifully finished walkout lower level.

Designed for both everyday comfort and effortless entertaining, the chef's kitchen features granite countertops, a spacious walk-in pantry, and a stylish butler's bar that seamlessly connects the dining room and breakfast nook. Just off the great room, a functional wet bar with beverage fridge adds the perfect touch for hosting gatherings.

Upstairs, four bedrooms include walk-in closets and ensuites, providing comfort and privacy for family and guests alike. The luxurious primary suite offers serene pond views, a spa-inspired bath with soaking tub and separate shower, and an expansive walk-in closet.

The finished walkout lower level is an entertainer's dream — complete with a full bar, fireplace lounge area, fifth bedroom, full bath, and private sauna. Step outside to enjoy the beautifully maintained, swimmable pond — ideal for fishing, paddle boating, canoeing, or simply relaxing along the water's edge.

Additional highlights include main-floor laundry, a 3-car attached garage with direct access to both the main and lower levels, and flexible living spaces perfectly suited for growing or multi-generational families.

With upscale finishes, generous living areas, and a tranquil waterfront setting close to everything, this home offers the ideal blend of luxury and lifestyle.
